The Journey of a Click

Every time we stream a video or load a page, data makes a physical journey through a global network of cables and cell towers.1 This practically invisible travel accounts for a significant portion of our personal carbon footprint as humans.4

  • The Cost of Quality: Higher resolution video requires more work from the network, leading to higher electricity consumption per minute.4
  • Mindful Connections: Choosing how we connect can drastically change our digital impact on the planet.4
