Defining Our Impact

To understand how the web affects our planet, it is important to grasp key concepts that shape what we now know about website usage and how and what it affects. These concepts shift our focus from the continuous and instantaneous availability of technology to continue to nurture and protect the planet we live on.

Concept Definition
Digital Seasonalism The practice of being aware of our data usage, only consuming what we need currently, rather than storing heavy, unused files indefinitely.
The Hidden Root System The energy intensive, physical network of data centers that are the core of our digital world.
Low-Bloom Design A web development approach that avoids high-contrast appearances and heavy media and instead opting for code that embodies a resilient and lightweight nature.
Data Composting The process of regularly and intentionally trashing digital items and/or information that are no longer in use to effectively reduce the energy that is required to store them.
Digital Stewardship The responsibility we have as humans who live on Earth to care for it by making browsing choices that are both mindful and sustainable.
