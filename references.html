Research Bibliography

The following resources provided the academic and technical data used throughout this research project:

  1. IEA (2024): Data Centres and Data Transmission Networks. International Energy Agency. [Source]
  2. Sustainable Web Design (2024): Principles of Sustainable Web Design. Co-authored by Wholegrain Digital and Mightybytes. [Source]
  3. The Green Web Foundation: Greening the Internet: Renewable Infrastructure. Open-source directory of green hosting. [Source]
  4. The Shift Project (2019): Lean ICT: Towards Digital Sobriety. Analysis of the global environmental impact of digital technologies. [Source]
  5. Carbon Trust (2021): Carbon Impact of Video Streaming. Technical briefing on energy intensity in global networks. [Source]

Tools & Learning Resources

Practical tools used to measure the "Materiality" and "Low-Bloom" nature of this website:

  • Website Carbon Calculator: Used to estimate the CO2 emissions per page load for this project. Visit Tool
  • W3C Web Sustainability Guidelines (WSG): The emerging international standard for eco-friendly web development. Read Guidelines
  • GreenGeeks Eco-Hosting Blog: A resource for understanding the transition to wind-powered web servers. Explore Blog
